How to backup Whatsapp?

There are many good reasons to regularly backup your WhatsApp messages. For one, it ensures that you won't lose any important conversations or media if you ever lose access to your phone or if it is damaged. Additionally, backing up your WhatsApp messages can be useful if you want to switch to a new phone or if you want to keep a record of your conversations for personal or professional purposes.

Steps to backup Whatsapp

  • 1.Open WhatsApp

  • 2. Click on three dots icon on the right side.

  • 3. Go to Setting

  • 4. Open Chats option

  • 5. Open Chat Backup

  • 6. Click Backup Button



Keeping a backup of our chats, photos, videos, and documents is important. Whatsapp helps us to do the same. It is very useful in case we change our smartphone to a new one. We can keep the backup and restore it in our new smartphone. Hope this was helpful. I look forward to sharing more in the future. Stay in touch to stay updated.
